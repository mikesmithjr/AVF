  5. 5. What percentage of Android users have the latest/greatest OS in the U.S? in Japan? in Great Britain? How might these differences impact development?

    The latest version of Android is 4.1 aka Jelly Bean which only has a 1.8% global market share. Android 2.3.3 aka Gingerbread is the most popular with about a 47% market share. I did see some fluctuations in those numbers but I have given an average of what I found. As far as impacting development I would say that even though we would all like to take advantage of the latest version and use its features in our apps it would be unwise to ignore the fact that most Android users are still on an older version of the software.
